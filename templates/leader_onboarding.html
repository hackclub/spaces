When members receive your code, they should:

  1. Log in to their Hack Club Spaces account (or create one)
  2. Navigate to the Club section in their dashboard
  3. Click "Join a Club" and enter your join code

Pro Tip

You can also add Co-Leaders to help you manage your club. Co-Leaders have similar privileges to Club Leaders, allowing them to manage members, create assignments, and more.

3

Plan Your First Meeting

Use the Schedule feature to plan and announce your first club meeting:

  1. Navigate to the Schedule tab in your club dashboard
  2. Click "Create New Meeting" and fill in the details
  3. Set a date, time, location, and meeting agenda
  4. Publish the meeting to notify all club members

Your members will receive notifications about the upcoming meeting, ensuring good attendance.

4

Create Your First Coding Challenge

Engage your members with a fun coding assignment:

  1. Go to the Assignments tab in your dashboard
  2. Click "New Assignment" to create a challenge
  3. Set a title, description, due date, and difficulty level
  4. Choose whether it's for all members or specific individuals
  5. Publish the assignment

Members can submit their solutions through Spaces, and you can review and provide feedback on their work.

Helping New Members Get Started

For club members who don't have a Spaces account yet, here's a simple guide to help them join:

1

Create a Spaces Account

Members should visit spaces.hackclub.com and click "Sign Up" to create a new account. They'll need to provide:

  • A username
  • Email address
  • Password
2

Join Your Club

After logging in, members should:

  1. Click on "Clubs" in the navigation
  2. Select "Join a Club"
  3. Enter your club's join code
  4. Click "Join Club"

Important Note

Make sure to share your club's join code with your members securely. The code provides direct access to your club.

3

Create Their First Project

Encourage members to create their first project on Spaces:

  1. After joining the club, go to Dashboard
  2. Click "New Space" to create a project
  3. Choose between a web project or a Python project
  4. Start coding!

All member projects will be visible in your Club Dashboard under the Projects tab, where you can feature outstanding work.
